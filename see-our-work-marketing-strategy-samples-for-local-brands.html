Helping Maya Grow Her Side Hustle into a Regenerative Microbrand

Client: Maya, founder of Maya’s Microgreens

Business Type: Home-based food producer, farmer’s market vendor

Stage: Early-stage side hustle, no digital presence yet

Discovery Highlights

  • Maya wants to grow beyond the market and build recurring orders
  • She’s overwhelmed by Instagram and unsure if a website is worth it
  • She values authenticity, sustainability, and her connection to local customers

Her biggest question: "How do I start marketing without losing my soul?"

Next → Calm + Credible Plan

A Calm + Credible Plan for Local Visibility

Strategy Goals

  • Establish a simple, one-page website with a fresh local feel
  • Use free tools like Google Business and a Linktree-style page to start
  • Set up reusable content templates (weekly market post, FAQ highlight, etc.)

How I Helped

Mini Brand Strategy

Defined values, audience, and a tone that felt true to her.

Visibility Plan

Set up a local SEO starter kit and made a map of easy wins.

Content Templates

Created 3 go-to posts she can reuse weekly with minimal effort.

What I’d Do: DFY + DWY Support

Done-For-You

  • Set up Maya’s website, Google profile, and a branded Linktree
  • Create her first 4 reusable posts and show her how to schedule them
  • Write a short About Me and Market FAQ based on our intake

Done-With-You

  • Guide Maya through posting weekly with gentle check-ins
  • Help her track which content gets the most traction
  • Co-write her first email or flyer for market handouts

This gentle start would give Maya momentum without burnout.
